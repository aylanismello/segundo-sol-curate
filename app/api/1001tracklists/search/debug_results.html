Welcome to 1001Tracklists Search!

It seems your search term didn't quite hit the mark. Don't worry, there's plenty more to explore. Here's a breakdown of what you can discover using our search feature:

Tracklists

Dive into comprehensive lists of tracks played by DJs in various sets, mixes, podcasts, and live performances. Each tracklist - or a simlar common term is playlist - offers you the played track names together with source where you can find or buy the track, like YouTube, SoundCloud, Apple Music, Beatport, Spotify, Traxsource, Bandcamp and more. Most of our tracklists also offer a full recording of the set or a link where you can find it. Our platform offers an extensive database of tracklists spanning different genres, allowing you to uncover the songs behind your favorite mixes or sets.
A good option for tracklists searches are the name of an artist and/or the name of a festival, location or podcast.

Labels

Discover record labels and their catalog of releases, ranging from underground gems to chart-topping hits. Stories

Immerse yourself in artist interviews, behind-the-scenes stories, and exclusive insights into the music industry. From in-depth conversations with established DJs to emerging talents, 1001Tracklists offers a glimpse into the personalities and experiences shaping the world of electronic music.
